Understand your Insurance obligation

Getting the highest quality advice is critical when deciding on Professional Indemnity Insurance. Speak to our team to understand your Insurance obligations under RG126, including;

  • The amount of policy limit coverage you should buy
  • The term “adequacy” within RG126 and the implications on you and your practice
  • Investor directed portfolio service (IDPS) operators (RG148)
  • Managed Discretionary Account services (RG179)

When applying for Professional Indemnity with us,
you should expect the following process:

  1. Prepare necessary documents and information, such as:
    AFSLs, FSG, SOA, Compliance Audit Report and Complaints Register.
  2. Complete the application process using our online form (30-45 minutes approx.)
  3. Arrange a video call with our team to discuss your Insurance requirements and answer any questions.
  4. Our team will review your information, select the most appropriate insurers, and negotiate for the best coverage and pricing. (within two weeks)
  5. Receive a Numerisk Insurance Proposal Report for your consideration via email.
  6. Discuss the proposal report with one of our experienced team members who can answer any queries you may have. If you choose to accept the quotations in your report, we will arrange for your cover to be placed.
